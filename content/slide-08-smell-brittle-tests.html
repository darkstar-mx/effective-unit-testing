Smell 1 Brittle Tests Coupled to Internals

Symptoms

  • Refactors break many tests.
  • Private method coverage drives test shape.
  • Renaming or reordering logic causes failures.

Fix

  • Test through public APIs.
  • Assert outcomes and invariants.
  • Delete tests that only mirror implementation.

When the test knows too much about the implementation, the test becomes part of the problem.
