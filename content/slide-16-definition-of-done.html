Definition of Done For Unit Tests in This Team

WRITEProtect behavior

Every new test must defend a business rule or contract.

REVIEWReject noise

We do not merge brittle or low-signal tests to satisfy a metric.

REFACTORImprove design

When testing is painful, we consider design changes part of the work.

TRUSTKeep CI credible

Test failures must deserve immediate attention.
