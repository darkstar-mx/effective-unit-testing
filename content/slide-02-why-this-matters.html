Why This Matters Senior Teams Pay the Tax

1

A small production change lands safely in local verification.

2

CI reports many failures unrelated to business behavior.

3

The team loses trust in tests, reviews get weaker, and refactoring slows down.

Bad outcome

Tests become noise, so engineers learn to work around them.

Target outcome

Tests fail only when expected behavior changes or a real defect is introduced.

The goal is not more tests. The goal is trustworthy feedback.
